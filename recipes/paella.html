Paella

A tasty spanish dish

This dish, based on rice, has its origins in the spanish city of Valencia. It has been prepared differently over the years, and even though the original dish contains rabbit meat, "batxoqueta", azafran and chicken, other ingredients have been added, like fish or peas. However, most valencians don't agree with this.

The recipe explained below is not the original one. Nevertheless, it tastes spectacular, too!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chorizo
  • Shrimp/li>
  • Rice
  • vegetables
  • Chicken stock
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken. Allow the chicken to marinate in olive oil, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper in the fridge for a few hours (or overnight).
  2. Cook the Rice. Heat olive oil in a paella pan and stir in garlic, red pepper flakes, and rice. Add saffron, bay leaf, parsley, chicken stock, and lemon zest. Simmer rice for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook the Protein and Vegetables. Cook chicken, onion, bell pepper, chorizo, and shrimp until done. Then add to the rice mixture.
