Lasagna

An italian pasta dish

This type of pasta, likely to be one of the oldest ones, is popular in the entire world. Because of its simplicity and unexpensive preparation, it is often chosen as a meal for both lunch and dinner.

Usually, it has several layers of cheese (like ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), vegetables, spices, and meat.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper for taste
  • 16 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint Ricotta cheese
